Keras Progress Bars¶

The following examples show two different keras_tqdm progress bars.

  • TQDM notebook widget
  • TQDM console output

To use keras_tqdm progress bars in your own code, just add TQDMCallback or TQDMNotebookCallback to the callbacks passed to model.fit.
